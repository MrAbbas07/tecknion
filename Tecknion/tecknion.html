About Us

Architect G. Chandra Shekar Reddy

Architect G. Chandra Shekar Reddy has significant experience in the field of architecture & interior designing on a wide range of projects. He worked with Simaha Associates before setting up Slicktech Architecture and Interiors.

Architect Rehmat Ali

Architect Rehmat Ali has significant experience on diversified & wide-ranging projects related to architecture & interiors. He has worked with Architects Atelier in Hyderabad before setting up Slicktech Architecture and Interiors.

The design team at Slicktech provides high-quality professional services in architecture and specializes in bespoke interior designing. We know that well-designed spaces lead to better working environments, and our design solutions strive for that balance. We make every effort to enhance the look and feel of the surroundings, which in turn brings out the best of human endowments in productivity and leisure, as we understand that space around us requires optimum utilization.

Our Firm Slicktech Architecture & Interiors was founded in 2003 and has successfully grown into a multi-disciplinary team comprising architects, designers, and civil engineers providing comprehensive advice for bespoke architecture and interiors. At Slicktech, technology and creativity go hand in hand, resulting in client satisfaction at all stages of the project. Our core strength is in space management, land use utilization, site analysis, and contemporary/modern architecture. We specialize and advise on residences, apartments, commercial complexes, hotels, resorts, and farmhouses. Every one of our projects is bespoke and speaks of our commitment in terms of design and aesthetics.

Tecknion Interiors is a sister concern of Slicktech Architects established in 2007. Tecknion deals exclusively in interior projects, from conceptualization to project completion.
